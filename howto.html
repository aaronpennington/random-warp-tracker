Controls

  • Left click a navigation item to load that world.
  • Hover over a warp point to see where it leads.
  • Left click a warp point to travel through it.
  • Right click a warp point to link them to other warps.

Map Legend

Below is a sample of Oldale Town.
  • White warps are unlinked.
  • Blue warps are in a 2-way link with another warp point.
  • Cyan warps are in a 1-way link with another warp point (You can enter Cyan, but not return through it).
  • Black warps are a dead end.
  • Gold warps are a key location.
