Randomized Warp Tracker

Controls

  • Left click a navigation item to load that item.
  • Left click a warp point to travel through it.
  • Double click a warp point to create a 2-way link to another point. Repeat on another point to bind the warp points.
  • Right click a warp point to create a 1-way link to another point. Repeat on another point to bind the warp points.
